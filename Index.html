About Us

Shecare is a groundbreaking digital support network tailored specifically for girls at APSIT. It offers a secure, inclusive, and empowering environment where students can connect, share, and flourish. Understanding the unique challenges and opportunities faced by APSIT girls, Shecare is dedicated to bridging gaps in support and resources. Our mission is to cultivate a community that is well-informed, connected, and confident, ensuring every girl has the support she needs to succeed.
